Fortnite For Noobs

The ultimate guide for survival (and just that)!

Do you suck at fornite ? Have zero kills and is always killed? We are here to help! This is a Guide on how to camp like a pro ! just follow these steps and you'll be a pro noob in no time ! for more tips watch this clip

step one - landing

  1. DO NOT land around the middle of the map or any big city/town
  2. Land far away towards the end of the map
  3. Try to land high up or on abandoned houses

step two - collecting

  1. Avoid collecting close range guns because they require close combat and we are NOT going to do that.
  2. Good guns are :
    • Snipers
    • Assault rifles
    • Supressed guns
  3. Find bandages, shield potions and medical kits
  4. Building resources: All of them!
  5. Bushes- for all you bush campers out there!

step three - suviving the storm

  1. Stay close to the edge of the storm but plan your route ahead so you dont get stuck (beware of cliffs and mountains)
  2. Use autorun when running from the storm
  3. Get your bandages ready! It might just save your life!
